Comprehensive Landscaping Solutions

Tailored services for residential and commercial properties.

1. Lawn Maintenance

Keep your property pristine year-round. Our routine maintenance includes precision mowing, trimming, edging, and organic fertilization to ensure deep roots and vibrant color.

2. Gardening & Planting

From seasonal flower bed rotations to pruning and weed management, our horticultural experts design and maintain gardens that thrive in Hampshire's specific climate.

3. Landscape Design & Installation

Bring your vision to life. We work with you to design sustainable, functional, and breathtaking landscapes, featuring drought-resistant native plants and eco-friendly mulch.

4. Hardscaping

Expand your living space outdoors. We design and install durable, elegant patios, walkways, retaining walls, and custom stone features.

5. Seasonal Services

Covoe protects and beautifies your property in the off-season with rapid-response snow removal and professional holiday lighting design and installation.
