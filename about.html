About Covoe

Founded and operated by Mr. Gift Nyamadzawo, Covoe Landscaping and Gardening was born from a simple philosophy: The beauty of nature is love. We believe that your outdoor space is an extension of your home or business—a place to relax, entertain, and connect with the natural world.

Based in Liphook, Hampshire, United Kingdom, we are a fully licensed and insured Limited Liability Company (LLC) dedicated to elevating the standard of landscaping. Unlike traditional landscaping companies, we prioritize the environment. By investing in fuel-efficient fleet vehicles, electric and battery-powered tools, and recycled materials, we minimize our carbon footprint while maximizing the curb appeal of your property.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, sustainable landscaping services that build lasting relationships with our clients and foster a positive, eco-conscious community.
